# Autonomous Capacity Scaling in Optical Metro Access Networks via Hierarchical SDN Control: A Demonstration


**Authors:** _**L. Vettori**_, Ricardo Martínez, Ramon Casellas, F. Javier Vílchez, Josep Maria Fàbrega, Ricard Vilalta and Raul Muñoz.  
**Conference:** 2026 Optical Fiber Communications Conference and Exhibition (OFC), Los Angeles, CA, USA, 2026  


## Abstract

Telemetry is foundational for multi-layer SDN automation operations enabling real-time state awareness. This demonstration showcases a hierarchical controller exploiting cross-domain telemetry data to autonomously trigger optical-layer capacity upgrades and resolve congestion, ensuring service assurance.
